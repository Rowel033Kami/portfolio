About Me.

Hi, I’m Rowel Lim, a dedicated and motivated Computer Science student with a strong passion for creating efficient, user-focused digital solutions. I enjoy turning complex ideas into clear, functional systems — whether through web development, software engineering, or hands-on hardware projects. I’m driven by continuous learning and the challenge of solving real-world problems through thoughtful design and clean, maintainable code.
